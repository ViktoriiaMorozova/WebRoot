None of us has ever worked with APIs, so the main challenge at the beginning of our work was figuring out how to implement this requirement. It was also challenging to divide work fairly and equally among us, considering each team member's strong and weak points. One more challenge relating to the code was discovered before a presentation. The bug was caused by an incorrect comparison of dates. It was discovered when we attempted to book with a check-in date of 2022-12-02 and a check-out date of 2022-12-10. The original code converted numbers without padding them with ‘0’: the day 1 is converted to a string “1”, instead of “01”.

As our next step, we should consider making our search form more detailed so that customers can perform a more specific search query. Since we ran out of time and completely forgot about our promise to be ADA-compliant, the next step is to make our application accessible to all users.
